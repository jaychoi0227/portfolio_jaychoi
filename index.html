Jay Choi

Music Director/Music Composer/Music Producer/Singer

Introduction




He started his music study as a voice major in Juilliard Pre-college in 2011 to 2014. He had won the 2nd place in Schumidt Vocal competition in 2013, and had chance to collaborate concert with Juilliard Orchestra in 2014. After graduating Juilliard, he got accepted in New England Conservatory of Music (NEC) in Boston, MA. In 2015, he has performed "Phantom of the Opera" in boston, and he also has performed the "Natioanl Anthem of United States, and Republic of Korea" at The Metropolitan Museum. He has studied voice for two years in NEC, and took a year off and became a music instructor in a music institute. He decided to stay in New York, and transferred to Adelphi University in Garden City. He has got in the Tau Sigma Honor Society, and awarded highest grant scholarship in the music department.







Career




  • Music Director of New York Friends Church since 2018

  • 2017 Tau Sigma National Honor Society
